1. Introduction

Important: The app is primarily offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from you to any server.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data, including:

  • Name, email, phone number, or any contact details
  • Device ID, IP address, or advertising ID
  • Real-time Location: We do NOT use GPS or network-based tracking. You select your district manually.

The app uses certain technical data locally on your device:

  • Device Sensors: The Accelerometer and Magnetometer are used solely for the Qibla Compass feature. This data is processed in real-time and is never stored or shared.
  • Local Preferences: Your chosen district and daily hadith status are saved using Android SharedPreferences to ensure the app works correctly when you reopen it.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following minimum permissions to function:

  • SET_ALARM: To allow you to set reminders for Sehri, Iftar, or Prayer times via your device's clock.
  • VIBRATE: To provide tactile feedback when the Qibla Compass is correctly aligned and for other user interactions.

4. How We Use Information

Since we collect no personal data, we do not use information for advertising, marketing, or profiling. Local data is used only to:

  • Display accurate timings for your manually selected district.
  • Function the Tasbih Counter and Qibla Finder.
  • Show a different authentic hadith every day.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share, sell, or disclose any information because no personal data is ever collected or transmitted. There are no third-party analytics or tracking libraries included in the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. As we plan to expand the app into a full Islamic platform after Ramadan, any changes regarding data usage will be clearly communicated and updated here.
